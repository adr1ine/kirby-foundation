the kirby foundation has humble roots, just like any other charity.




our founders were inspired by kirby's ability to transform into whatever foods that he ate, and that got them wondering -- was there any way to recreate that in real life, considering the ramifications that could possibly have for solving hunger around the world?


well, fifteen years later, we had our answer: probably not.


but that doesn't mean that we stopped trying to solve pay gaps, pay inequalities, and hunger. we just took it in a different direction and after some thinking, we realized: why not just go back to our roots?

that was when the kirby foundation was truly reborn -- by turning a popular american video game character into a symbol of charity (and by partnering with its parent company, nintendo), we were able to help raise awareness about these issues, and that brings us to today.


we need your help.

even if you can't donate or buy one of our products or products of our partners, spreading the word is just as good. after all, everyone can contribute!
for more info on how to help, email our address reserved for this exact purpose, at kirby.foundation.questions@kfound.org
