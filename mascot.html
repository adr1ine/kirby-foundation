well, when did this kirby-mania start?

kirby was first introduced as the protagonist in an action-platform game that was developed by hal laboratory and released by nintendo, first released in 1992.






then what did kirby do in this game?

his main abilities in-game include inhaling any object, even if it is larger than him, and afterwards, he is able to spit it out as a projectile or to swallow it.



kirby resides in a kingdom called dream land, on a planet that is shaped like a five-pointed star, called popstar, hence his typical depiction being alongside a star or multiple stars. popstar is also home to creatures called waddle dees, who occasionally appear as enemies, but most regularly as a friend of kirby.





why should i care, though? he's just a little pink blob.

  1. kirby games have been some of the most successful games out there; most have sold over a million copies, and as a result, kirby is one of the most easily recognizable video game characters in modern pop culture.

  2. and thus, for those who wonder why we use kirby as our mascot? it's because he's an icon who represents what all children deserve: food, innocence, happiness, and most of all, the experience of childhood.
